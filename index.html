The Future Turtles are a disorganized bunch of friends camping together at Burning Man since 2018 without any drama, death, or injury.

More

Who we are

We started in 2018 as the Gay Turtle Makeout, a group of friends from the tech industry mostly from New York and San Francisco. In 2019 we grew to 18 people including campers from Los Angeles and London and plenty of non-techies too. We're all super nice, low-drama, hard working, and kinda hot. Oh, and modest!

Next

How we camp

We build an awesome camp every year and share a lot of things like Shiftpods, a great shade structure, a kitchen with two communal meals a day, water and grey water disposal, a very exhibitionist shower situation (camp is clothing optional), a generator, free snacks, tools, and more. We don't allow RVs or private vehicles. We definitely don't allow drama, sparkle-ponies, or people who don't work hard.

Interactivity

Future Turtles is a placed theme camp at 4:15 and E, in the heart of the emergency backup gayborhood, which is, by the way, the BEST gayborhood. We run extensive interactive programming throughout the week.

Camp with us

We only accept members who have a Future Turtle sponsor, which means, you already know people from the camp. If you're interested in camping with us, reach out to the people from the camp that you know and get on the mailing list. Then follow the mailing list and social media, especially our Facebook page. Come to our meetups and get to know the members. Please feel free to reach out, come to our parties during the year or visit us on the playa!
