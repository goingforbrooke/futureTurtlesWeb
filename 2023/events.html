Welcome to the Queerborhood Sunset Block Party Su 7 PM - 9 PM Welcome home! This year the absolute ground zero of the queerborhood is at 7:00 and Encantado. We looked it up, and an Encantado is a mythical pink, sparkly were-dolphin known for their irresistible allure and enchanting qualities. Well, that sounds a lot like the Future Turtles, so we think we’ve found our home. Come to our opening sunset party, meet the Future Turtles, and find your long-lost friends, your next playa boyfriend, and more. Check out the view from our observation decks, explore the Pillow Fort and Cuddle Fort, and have a drink (alcoholic or non). As befits a camp from the future, our DJs commit to not playing the same old blah blah pop songs.

Ürkish Coffee Tu Th 11:59pm - 1am Allow yourself to be drawn into a world of rich traditions and mystique, where a cup of Ürkish Coffee, and many-a-Turtles to befriend awaits you. This is no ordinary brew, mind you, but an exotic elixir born from the exclusive beans of Black Rock Farms. Once you have relished the last drop, ask for the “kahin”, a seer guided by the divine dance of coffee grounds. Lend us your cup, and we shall weave tales of your future from the sediment left behind. But tread lightly, dear visitor, for the paths revealed by the coffee's remnants are not easily erased. The future they whisper can be as intoxicating as the brew itself, and once disclosed to you, they imprint themselves onto the canvas of destiny. Come for coffee and friends, and if you dare, leave with the knowledge of your destiny…
Turtle Lounge Mo Tu We Th Fr Sa 11 AM - 11 PM At the center of the Queerborhood, the enchanting and alluring Future Turtles have built a gorgeous space to hang out 24/7. Whether you are on your way out for an evening on playa, coming back after a tiring bike ride, or looking to chill out and meet your neighbors, you’re welcome to pop in any time. We have plenty of comfortable seating. There’s an air conditioned Cuddle Fort for cuddle puddles, and a separate air conditioned Pillow Fort for consensual male play. This year we’ve added a new observation deck with great views of the neighborhood and the playa. Because the Future Turtles believe Burning Man is a magical place outside of normal space and time, we think that nothing on playa should accidentally wrench you back to the default world. So our DJs commit to never playing popular music or really anything you’ve heard before.
